UX Case Study:

Imagine you’ve just been hired as a product designer for a new travel startup. The startup’s goal is to build a mobile app to help the modern traveler to plan their next trip

The Final Product:

The EXP TRVL app allows for travelers to automatic sync their travel plans to their calendar.

ROLE & RESPONSIBILITIES

UX/UI Designer: User Research, Interaction, Visual Design

Programs Utilized: Figma, Miro, InVision, Adobe Creative Suite

Timeline: 4 weeks

Problem Statement

Travelers are often pressed for time while on vacation which makes it difficult to stay on schedule.

Solution Statement

Create an app that is able to book excursions and experiences and have them automatically sync to her calendar so the user can be reminded when to go to their next destination.

User Insight

Travelers have difficulty planning and booking excursions for their vacations that automatically sync to their calendars. While conducting interviews, I found that many travelers found it cumbersome to manually input their vacation excursions into their calendars. They also wanted to receive alerts to their plans as well as to make sure they are on time. Therefore I believe that trip itinerary calendar synchronization will relieve stress and enhance traveler's vacation experiences. I may be able to help if I build an app that automatically syncs excursions and trip details to the travelers’ calendar.

User Persona

After putting together date from user interviews and creating an affinity diagram, I created this persona using the Empathy Map I created based off the user interviews I conducted to understand how users plan their trips and experiences.
